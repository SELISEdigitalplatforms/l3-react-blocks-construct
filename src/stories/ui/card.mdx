<Subtitle>Overview</Subtitle>

Cards are flexible and extensible content containers that include options for headers, footers, and a variety of content types.

## Variants

- **Default:** Standard card with header, content, and footer.
- **Image:** Card with an image at the top.
- **Horizontal:** Card with content beside the image.

## Props

- **header:** Content displayed at the top of the card.
- **footer:** Content displayed at the bottom of the card.
- **image:** URL of the image to display.
- **title:** Title of the card.
- **content:** Main content of the card.

## Best Practices

- Use cards to group related content.
- Ensure images are relevant and of high quality.
- Keep content concise and to the point.
- Use footers for actions related to the card's content.

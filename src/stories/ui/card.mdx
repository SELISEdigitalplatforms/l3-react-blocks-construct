## Variants

- **Default:** Standard card with header, content, and footer.
- **Image:** Card with an image at the top.
- **Horizontal:** Card with content beside the image.

## Best Practices

- Use cards to group related content.
- Ensure images are relevant and of high quality.
- Keep content concise and to the point.
- Use footers for actions related to the card's content.

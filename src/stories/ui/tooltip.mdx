## Variants

### States & Behavior

Tooltips can appear in different configurations:

- **Default** — basic tooltip for general use.
- **With Delay** — appears after a short delay for less disruptive UX.
- **Different Sides** — can be positioned on top, bottom, left, or right of the trigger element.

## Accessibility

- Trigger tooltips with both **hover** and **keyboard focus**.
- Provide fallback descriptions with `aria-label` if the tooltip contains critical information.
- Tooltips should disappear when users press **Esc** or move focus away.

## When to Use

- To explain icons, acronyms, or truncated content.
- To provide extra context without cluttering the UI.
- Avoid using tooltips for essential instructions — that belongs in labels or inline help text.

## Best Practices

✅ **Do**

- Keep tooltip text concise (one sentence or less).
- Position tooltips so they don’t obstruct the element.
- Use them to clarify, not replace, labels.

❌ **Don’t**

- Place critical or interactive content inside tooltips.
- Rely only on hover — always support keyboard focus.
